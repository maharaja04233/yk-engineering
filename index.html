Frequently Asked Questions

A comprehensive resource tailored to address your most common queries and deliver valuable insights into our fabrication and erection services, project capabilities, and the evolving industrial construction landscape.

What types of structures and services do you offer as a fabrication and erection contractor?

As a fabrication and erection contractor, we specialize in the design, fabrication, and on-site erection of steel structures including industrial sheds, pipe racks, platforms, storage tanks, pressure vessels, and custom steel assemblies for various sectors such as oil & gas, power, infrastructure, and manufacturing.

How can I request your fabrication and erection services?

You can get started by contacting our team with your project specifications or drawings. We'll review your requirements, provide a detailed quotation, and guide you through the next steps, from design approval to on-site erection.

Do you offer custom fabrication based on client drawings?

Yes, we provide custom fabrication services based on client-supplied drawings or can collaborate during the design phase to develop buildable, cost-effective structural solutions.
